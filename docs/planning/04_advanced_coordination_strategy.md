## 3. (A + B): The "Sustainable Duo" (Prototype Priority)
Consolidating **Volunteer Fatigue** and **Mega-Squad Splitting** provides a much clearer explanation of how our system works in tandem for a high-impact demo.

### A. Volunteer "Burnout" & Fatigue Tracking (The "Vibe Check")
- **The Concept:** Each volunteer starts with **100 Energy**.
- **The Logic:** Every mission reduces Energy by **25**. When it hits **0**, they aren't suggested anymore (they are "forced to rest").
- **Hackathon Pitch:** *"Our AI protects the mental health and physical stamina of our heroes to prevent burnout in long-term disasters."*

### B. Multi-Lead "Mega-Squads" (Team Alpha & Beta)
- **The Concept:** If victims are **>40**, the AI realizes one group of 10 is too hard to manage.
- **The Logic:** It automatically splits the volunteers into **Team Alpha** and **Team Beta**, each with its own specialized Lead.
- **Goal:** This shows **"Organizational Intelligence"**—ensuring manageable tactical control and double the leadership presence on the ground.

### Why they belong together:
- **Total Coverage:** Mega-Squads solve the problem for the **Victims** (Better organization), while Fatigue Tracking protects the **Volunteers** (Prevents burnout).
- **The Operational Loop:** When a massive disaster struck, the AI splits into Alpha & Beta. Upon assignment, both teams lose energy. Eventually, these members get "Exhausted," forcing the AI to cycle in fresh responders for the next deployment.
