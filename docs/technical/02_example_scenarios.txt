========================================
  EXAMPLE SCENARIOS: AI DECISION MAKING
========================================

SCENARIO A: THE MEDICAL EMERGENCY
---------------------------------
TASK: "Elderly man collapsed, needs doctor now!"
LOCATION: (5, 5)

Potential Volunteers:
1. Doc A: Skills [Health], Location (100, 100) - FAR
2. Doc B: Skills [Health], Location (5.5, 5.5) - CLOSE
3. Helper C: Skills [General], Location (5.1, 5.1) - VERY CLOSE

AI LOGIC RESULT:
--> Doc B is ranked #1. 
--> Why? Because Doc B has the "Health" skill (High Priority) AND is close enough to help. 
--> Helper C is ignored because they lack medical skills, even though they are closer.

SCENARIO B: FOOD DISTRIBUTION
-----------------------------
TASK: "Daily meals delivery needed."
LOCATION: (0, 0)

Potential Volunteers:
1. Vol A: Skills [Relief], Location (2, 2), Busy: Yes
2. Vol B: Skills [Relief], Location (5, 5), Busy: No

AI LOGIC RESULT:
--> Vol B is ranked #1.
--> Why? Even though Vol A is closer, they are MARKED AS BUSY. 
--> The AI prioritizes "Availability" so the task is actually done.
